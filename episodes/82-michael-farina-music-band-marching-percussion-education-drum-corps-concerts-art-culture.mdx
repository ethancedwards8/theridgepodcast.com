In this episode, Ethan interviews Mr. Michael Farina, musician and band teacher at Carroll County High School. They discuss his introduction to music, his education, first jobs in music, and what it takes to direct a marching band, covering the choreography and coordination of it all. They also discuss his time in the Drum Corps, the work that goes into it, and culture. Finally, they discuss his love and career in music, bands that he plays in, and more.



Thank you again Mr. Farina for being on the podcast.
