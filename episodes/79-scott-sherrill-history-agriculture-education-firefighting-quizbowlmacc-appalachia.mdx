<p>In this episode, Ethan interviews Mr. Scott Sherrill, history teacher at Carroll County Middle School, volunteer firefighter, and MACC coach. They discuss his early education, his passions for agriculture and history, and his decision to become an educator. They cover his involvement with the volunteer fire departments and coaching academic teams like Scholastic Bowl and MACC. Finally, they discuss local history, culture, and what it means to be an Appalachian.</p>



<p>Thank you again Mr. Sherrill for being on the podcast.</p>
